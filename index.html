Origin

Born From the
Desert.

In the heart of the Antelope Valley, where the Mojave sun is relentless and the wind carries grit, AV Boxing Club was built on a single belief: that discipline forged in adversity produces champions. From humble beginnings in Palmdale, this gym became a sanctuary — a place where fighters find purpose, direction, and excellence.

The founders saw a community that needed more than a place to throw punches. It needed a home. A place where young men and women could discover what boxing demands of you — and what it gives back. Where sweat on the canvas means something. Where every round teaches a lesson that extends far beyond the ring.

What started as a small operation with secondhand equipment and borrowed space grew into one of the Antelope Valley's most respected boxing programs — producing fighters, coaches, and community leaders alike. USA Boxing sanctioned. Community driven. Built to last.

"We don't just train fighters. We build people who can weather any storm life throws at them." — AV Boxing Club

History

Round by Round

The Beginning
Doors Open in the Antelope Valley

AV Boxing Club opens its doors in Palmdale, California. With little more than a ring, heavy bags, and a coach with a vision, the gym welcomes its first class of fighters — many of them young people with nowhere else to go and everything to prove.

Early Years
First Regional Competitors

The club enters Southern California's amateur circuits. Early fighters bring home hardware and earn respect from established programs across the region, putting AV on the boxing map.

Growth Era
Youth Program & Fists of Fury

The gym launches its signature youth program, keeping hundreds of young people in the AV focused and disciplined. The "Fists of Fury" amateur boxing show is born — a hometown event that gives local fighters a stage of their own.

Championship Years
Regional Titles & USA Boxing Sanctioned

AV Boxing Club gains full USA Boxing sanctioning. Fighters from the program begin appearing at state-level competitions, with coaches recognized as among the best in the Antelope Valley.

Today
A Community Institution

Now a pillar of the Palmdale community — serving the AV, Lancaster, and surrounding areas. Training competitive fighters and fitness members side by side, staying true to its founding mission: hard work, discipline, and a shot for anyone willing to put in the effort.
